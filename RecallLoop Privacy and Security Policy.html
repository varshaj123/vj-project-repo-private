1. Purpose of the Platform

RecallLoop is a digital cognitive screening and monitoring platform designed to:

n  Assess cognitive functions such as memory, attention, language, and processing speed.

n  Track cognitive performance over time.

n  Provide insights and trends related to cognitive wellness.

RecallLoop is not a diagnostic medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

2. Voluntary Participation

Your participation in RecallLoop is:

n  Completely voluntary.

n  You may stop using the platform at any time.

n  You may request deletion of your data.

3. Nature of the Assessments

The platform includes:

n  On-boarding questionnaires with self-reported lifestyle and health information.

n  Assessments for different Cognitive skills.

These are used for screening and tracking only.

Results:

n  Do not confirm the presence of dementia, MCI or any other cognitive disorder as such.

n  Should not replace consultation with a qualified healthcare professional.

If your results indicate elevated risk, you are encouraged to consult a licensed medical practitioner.

4. Data We Collect

RecallLoop may collect the data related to the following:

n  Basic demographic information (age, education, language).

n  Health information reported by the individual.

n  Cogintive performance data.

We do not collect unrelated personal data as part of RecallLoop.

5. Usage of Your Data

Your data may be used to:

n  Generate cognitive performance insights.

n  Improve platform accuracy.

n  Develop normative baselines.

n  Conduct anonymized research and product enhancement.

n  Your identifiable data will not be sold to any third parties.

6. Data Privacy & Security

RecallLoop will ensure the privacy and security of the data through:

n  Uses encryption for data transmission.

n  Stores data in secure cloud infrastructure.

n  Restricts access to authorized personnel only.

n  We comply with the applicable data protection laws.

7. Risks & Limitations

The potential risks may include:

n  Emotional discomfort if results suggest decline.

n  Misinterpretation of screening results.

n  Technology-related issues.

n  RecallLoop does not guarantee accuracy equivalent to clinical neuropsychological evaluation.

8. Age Requirements

To use the RecallLoop platform, the users must be:

n  18 years or older OR

n  Using the platform under supervision of a guardian or caregiver.

9. Disclaimer

RecallLoop is not the following:

n  It is not a substitute for medical advice.

n  Is not an alternative for a doctor.

n  Should not be used for emergency medical decisions.

n  If you experience any cognitive symptoms like memory loss, confusion or neurological symptoms then seek immediate medical care.
